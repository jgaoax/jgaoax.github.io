LGT3800

  • Aviation Finance (3 credits), The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, Fall 2025 (45 students)
  • This course delivers key concepts and analytical methods relevant to the aviation sector. It covers asset volatility, risk preferences, real-option analysis, fuel hedging, aircraft leasing, fintech applications, regulatory frameworks, cost of capital, and slot trading mechanisms.
